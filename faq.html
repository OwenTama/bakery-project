Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pastries, orders, and ingredients.

Do you offer gluten-free bread options?

While our authentic sourdough undergoes a long fermentation that breaks down a lot of gluten proteins making it easier to digest, it is still made from wheat flour. We do bake specialized flourless almond cakes, but we are not a certified gluten-free facility.

Can I place a large custom order for an event?

Yes! We love catering for events. Please use our form on the Contact page to submit your details at least 48 hours in advance so our baking schedule can accommodate your request.

What time do fresh loaves come out of the oven?

Our morning pastries and baguettes are ready right when we open at 7:00 AM. Our signature sourdough loaves exit the ovens around 8:30 AM every morning.
