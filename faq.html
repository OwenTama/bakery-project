Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pastries, custom orders, and ingredients in Nairobi.

Do you offer gluten-free bread options?

While our authentic sourdough undergoes a long fermentation process that breaks down many of the heavy gluten proteins to make it easier to digest, it is still baked from wheat flour. We offer special flourless almond pastries, but we are not a certified gluten-free facility.

Can I place a large custom order for an event?

Yes! We cater for corporate events and private gatherings across Nairobi. Please use the form on our Contact page to submit your details at least 48 hours in advance so our kitchen can prepare.

What time do fresh loaves come out of the oven?

Our morning pastries and breakfast croissants are ready right when we open at 7:00 AM. Our flagship signature artisan sourdough loaves emerge fresh from the deck ovens around 8:30 AM daily.

Do you deliver within Nairobi?

Yes! We partner with local delivery services to deliver fresh pastries directly to your doorstep across Kilimani, Westlands, and the wider Nairobi area every morning.
