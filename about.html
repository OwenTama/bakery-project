Our Story

Founded in 2020, The Daily Crumb started as a small backyard garage experiment with a single Dutch oven and a dream to bring authentic artisan bread to our local neighborhood.

We believe that good things take time. That is why our breads undergo a slow 24-hour cold fermentation process, allowing natural wild yeasts to maximize flavor and digestability. We partner directly with local millers to source sustainable, unbleached flour without any chemical preservatives.

Our Core Promise

"Never rushed, never artificial. Just flour, water, salt, and passion baked fresh every day."
