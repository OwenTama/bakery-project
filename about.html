Our Nairobi Story

Founded in 2020, The Daily Crumb started as a passionate kitchen experiment right here in Nairobi, aiming to introduce authentic sourdough to the local community.

We believe great bread requires patience. Our signature loaves undergo a slow 24-hour fermentation process using locally sourced, sustainable grains from Kenyan millers. We never use artificial preservatives, ensuring every bite is wholesome and naturally flavorful.

Our Promise

"Never rushed, never artificial. Just premium local ingredients, water, sea salt, and wild cultures baked fresh daily."
