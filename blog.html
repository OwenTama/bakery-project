Baking Tips

Why Time is Your Most Important Ingredient

Published on June 18, 2026 by Head Baker Clara

Commercial bakeries rely on chemical additives to force bread dough to rise in under an hour. At The Daily Crumb, we step backwards into history. By cooling our dough overnight for a slow 24-hour fermentation, wild bacteria break down complex starches natively, giving the bread an unforgettable deep flavor profile and making it much gentler on sensitive stomachs.

Want to unlock the secrets of baking premium wild yeast bread inside your own home oven?
